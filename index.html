The studio

We build like the platform is a place we live.

No frameworks bolted on, no trackers tucked in. Three things guide everything that leaves the lab.

Private by design

On-device first. We use Apple's frameworks and on-device intelligence so your data has no reason to leave your phone.

Native & fast

Built in SwiftUI for iPhone specifically — not a web app in a costume. Fluid, light on battery, instant to open.

Made to delight

Haptics, motion, sound, and the tiny details that earn a smile. Accessibility and Reduce Motion are baked in, not bolted on.

How we work

From a feeling to the App Store.

A short, honest path. You see real builds early and often — usually within the first week.

01

Spark

We name the single feeling the app should give, and the one person it's for.

02

Shape

Prototypes in days, designed in the open. We cut everything that doesn't serve the feeling.

03

Build

Native Swift, on-device, accessible by default. Tested on real devices, not just the simulator.

04

Launch

We sweat the screenshots, the metadata, and the day-one polish — then keep tending it.
